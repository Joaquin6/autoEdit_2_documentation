# Adobe Extensions HostList codes

`manifest.xml` , `<HostList>` list of possible values

```text
PHXS: Photoshop
PHSP: Photoshop (older code)
ILST: Illustrator
FLPR: Flash Pro
PPRO: Premiere Pro
AEFT: After Effects
PRLD: Prelude
IDSN: InDesign
AICY: InCopy
AUDT: Audition
```



from [CEP Guide Part 2: Getting Started](http://aphall.com/2014/08/cep-5-getting-started-en/)
